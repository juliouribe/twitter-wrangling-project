Insights and Visualization¶

Looking at the clean data set, we can see that about 79% of the tweets in this data set have valid ratings using a scale of having a rating at least of 10 because 'every dog is a good boy' and capping values to 19 so that we don't hyper inflate the mean with invalid values such as '420' or '1776'.

Average ratings¶

The average rating that a dog gets on the WeRateDogs twitter profile is 11.44. So while guaranteeing a value of 10, the evaluators don't tend to rate too much higher than that on average.

Conclusion¶

Overall, WeRateDogs is a feel good profile but they tend to be fairly consistent about their ratings. When a dog is present in the tweet, they will grade fairly and rarely use values higher than 13, only for the very good boys/girls.
